================================================================
WHY THESE WORK BETTER:
================================================================

❌ BAD: "organizational structure diagram"
   → Sounds like search query, not tutoring question
   → LLM gets confused about intent

✅ GOOD: "What is the organizational structure of a company?"
   → Natural student question
   → Clear intent to learn
   → LLM understands how to teach

❌ BAD: "accounting equation diagram"
   → Just keywords, no context

✅ GOOD: "What is the accounting equation? Explain with example"
   → Complete question
   → Clear what student wants to learn
   → Natural conversation flow


================================================================
TIPS FOR ASKING QUESTIONS:
================================================================

1. Start with: "What is...", "Explain...", "How does..."
2. Be specific about the topic
3. Ask as if talking to a real tutor
4. Avoid just keywords - use complete sentences

Examples:
✅ "What is money market and how is it different from capital market?"
✅ "Explain the organizational structure of companies"
✅ "How does the stock exchange process work?"
✅ "What are the main types of financial markets?"

❌ "money market diagram"
❌ "company structure chart"
❌ "balance sheet format"
